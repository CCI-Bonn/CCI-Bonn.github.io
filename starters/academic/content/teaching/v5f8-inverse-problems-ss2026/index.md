### Course concept

This course explores several common inverse problems in the life sciences, with a specific emphasis on applications in medical imaging and radiology. To address these challenges, the course introduces foundational concepts in optimization and covers efficient numerical algorithms tailored for solving these problems.

### Topics

1. Basic convex analysis.
2. Optimality conditions.
3. Constrained and unconstrained optimization.
4. Canonical convex programs.
5. Lagrangian duality, KKT conditions, minimax theorems.
6. Analysis of gradient method.
7. Applications in medical imaging.

### Reading material

1. [Convex Optimization](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/) by Stephen Boyd and Lieven Vandenberghe.
2. *Introductory Lectures on Convex Programming* by Y. Nesterov.
3. Lecture slides.
4. Various online resources.
