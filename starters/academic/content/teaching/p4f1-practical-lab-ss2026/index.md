## Teaching details

**Course code:** P4F1  
**Semester:** Summer 2026  
**Instructors:** Dr. Aditya Rastogi and Prof. Alexander Effland  
**Language of instruction:** English

### Course concept

Practical lab. This practical lab focuses on solving classical inverse imaging problems using Python. Students will implement algorithms for denoising, deblurring, and sparse reconstruction while exploring key concepts of convexity and optimization. The exercises include biomedical applications, where the developed methods are applied to reconstruct medical images such as MRI and CT.

### Topics

1. Medical image reconstruction (CT/MRI).
2. Medical image denoising.
3. Medical image deblurring.
4. Medical image inpainting.
5. Bayesian approach in medical imaging.

2. *Introductory Lectures on Convex Programming* by Y. Nesterov.
